About Cuddlefish

Cuddlefish is a revolutionary robotic avatar concept that enables safe physical interactions for people separated by distance.

We demonstrate a new robotic avatar in which pilots can embody a fish-like form, "swimming" through indoor spaces to interact, play, and accompany others remotely. Our soft flapping-wing floating avatar combines bioinspired design with cutting-edge technology to create lifelike, organic movements that enhance the sense of presence.

By mimicking the flapping flight of birds and the graceful swimming of aquatic creatures, pilots can control the avatar through intuitive body movements while engaging in meaningful interactions through the avatar's soft body and wings.

How People Interact

Cuddlefish evokes natural, affective responses from people, fostering warm emotional connections through physical interaction.

Touch & Stroke

71% of participants reached out to touch the robot, with many stroking its soft body or gently cupping it. The soft, approachable design invites natural tactile interaction.

Hug & Embrace

Participants spontaneously hugged the robot, describing it as "like a cute pet" and feeling warmth and affection. The robot's approach evoked desire to receive it with open arms.

Wave & Greet

When the robot waved its wings, people waved back, high-fived the wings, or grabbed them to "shake hands" - interpreting the movements as friendly greetings.

Catch & Support

As the robot descended, participants instinctively reached out to catch it, showing protective and caring behaviors typically reserved for living creatures.

Play & Explore

People jumped to touch the robot, tried to playfully "tease it back," and followed its movements with curiosity, engaging in spontaneous play behaviors.

Emotional Response

Participants reported feeling "peaceful," "warm," "charmed," and "touched" - demonstrating the robot's ability to evoke genuine emotional engagement and empathy.

Use Cases

Remote Family Connection

A parent working overseas could remotely control the avatar to play with their child at home. The child might chase or jump to touch the avatar floating in mid-air, sharing a joyful game of tag. Parents can use their "wings" to hug their child, enabling affectionate interactions that go beyond what screen-based communication can offer.

Telepresence & Social Interaction

Transform how people connect across distances by embodying a physical form in remote locations. Cuddlefish reshapes how we maintain relationships when physical separation or isolation creates barriers, enabling pilots to be truly present in shared spaces.

Performance & Art

The unique movement capabilities and expressive potential make Cuddlefish ideal for interactive performances, installations, and artistic expressions that blend technology with organic, lifelike motion.

Companionship & Care

The robot's pet-like qualities and ability to evoke warm emotional responses make it suitable for providing comfort and companionship to people who are isolated or in need of social connection.
